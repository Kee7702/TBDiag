Play Online or LAN Multiplayer without Crashing

It's common to crash when trying to join a friend, and this might fix that issue. It is important that you ask for permission from the owner of a world before you join with Toolbox.

Join a Local World

Create or play an existing world locally on your device, staying in the world for a short period of time.

Try Again

Go into the same world which caused crashing previously, and it should be possible to now load into the world without crashing.

Important Notice

Toolbox is not meant to be used for unfair advantages in any worlds, realms, or servers, especially without permission to use it. We will not help bypass anti-cheat.
